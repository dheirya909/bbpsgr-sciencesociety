About Navachar

Navachar is more than just a science exhibitionâ€”it's a celebration of curiosity, innovation, and the relentless pursuit of knowledge. This flagship event of the BBPS GRH Science Society brings together students, educators, and science enthusiasts to showcase cutting-edge projects spanning physics, chemistry, biology, robotics, and beyond.

From working models and experimental demonstrations to research presentations and interactive displays, Navachar provides a platform for young scientists to present their ideas, compete for recognition, and inspire their peers. The event fosters collaborative learning, critical thinking, and hands-on exploration of scientific concepts.
